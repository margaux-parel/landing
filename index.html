What is Travelone?

You want to travel alone but do not know how to do it, where to go or how to be safe while being alone? We got you! With Travelone you will be able to find safe places, routes created by tourists, locals and specialists in the sector. You will have all to good adresses and tips needed wherever you go. Thanks to our connected network you will meet other people from all around the world travelling alone during your trip. Travelone is first of all a human experience. You will discover new cultures by being closer to local populations by sharing your experiences!
